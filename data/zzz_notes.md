# ZZZ Mini Knowledge Base (Starter)

## Team planning basics
- Common roles: Rupture, Attack, Stun, Support, Anomaly, and Defense.
- A stable team usually has a damage dealer(Rupture, Attack, Anomaly) plus utility (Stun/Support/Defense) depending on your playstyle.
- Within the game there is plenty of varaince as well, with certain teams being viable depending on acquired characters, such as Double Attack Teams, or Double Anomaly Teams

## Resource tracking idea
- Track what you spend (materials, currency) and set weekly goals to avoid spreading upgrades too thin.

## Build notes example
- Write short notes like: target stats, rotation reminders, and what to farm next.
